This strips the ASCII punctuation `urlize` leaves in the path (`+`, `/`, ...) -- which a CDN like S3/CloudFront mis-decodes (a literal `+` becomes a space -> 404) -- while keeping Unicode letters, which `urlize` then percent-encodes (CDN-safe).
